# Confined Space Entry - Vessels and Tanks

**Document ID:** SP-003
**Version:** Rev 3
**Effective Date:** 2023-03-05
**Owner Role:** HSE Officer
**Classification:** Site Safety Procedure (mandatory compliance)

## 1. Purpose

This Safety Procedure establishes mandatory requirements for "Confined Space Entry - Vessels and Tanks" across all
process units at PRPC, in line with PESO, DISH (Factories Act 1948), and OISD guidance.

## 2. Applicability

This procedure applies to all personnel — PRPC employees and contract technicians —
performing work covered by this procedure. It currently governs 4
equipment items across the plant, including: 100-V-105, 200-PSV-207, 200-P-208, 400-T-409
.

## 3. Requirements

1. A valid permit-to-work must be issued and countersigned by the Shift Supervisor and
   HSE Officer before work commences.
2. All personnel involved must hold the relevant certification (see personnel roster
   `PERS-REG`) prior to participating in this activity.
3. Isolation, lockout-tagout, and verification steps must be completed and logged before
   any mechanical or electrical intervention.
4. In the event of a deviation or near-miss during execution, work must stop immediately
   and be reported per the incident reporting protocol.

## 4. Non-Compliance

Any incident arising from non-compliance with this procedure is investigated under the
plant's incident management process and may trigger a regulatory submission depending on
severity.
