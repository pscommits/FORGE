# Sludge Dewatering and Disposal

**Document ID:** SOP-500-015
**Version:** Rev 3
**Effective Date:** 2023-01-09
**Unit:** Effluent Treatment Plant (500)
**Owner Role:** Process Engineer

## 1. Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure defines the safe and consistent method for
"Sludge Dewatering and Disposal" within the Effluent Treatment Plant at Panchganga Refinery & Petrochemicals
Complex (PRPC).

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all operations and shift personnel responsible for equipment
governed by this SOP, including: 500-AGT-501, 500-P-502, 500-V-503, 500-AGT-504.

## 3. Roles &amp; Responsibilities

- **Shift Supervisor** — authorizes the start of the activity and confirms pre-requisites.
- **Panel Operator** — executes control room actions and monitors process parameters.
- **Field Operator** — performs field verification, valve line-up, and local checks.
- **Process Engineer** (Process Engineer) — owns this document and approves deviations.

## 4. Procedure Steps

1. Confirm permit-to-work and applicable safety procedure isolation status before starting.
2. Verify instrumentation is in calibration and control loops are in the correct mode.
3. Execute the sequence in accordance with the unit's control philosophy, monitoring
   critical parameters against design limits at each step.
4. Record all readings in the shift log and report deviations to the Shift Supervisor
   immediately.
5. On completion, confirm final equipment status and update the equipment's operating log.
